About Me

Hi, my name is Gregory Jaros. I was born in Providence RI, but grew up in North Carolina with a family of three brothers and two great parents. Growing up I enjoyed working with tech and software, taking electives such as lego robotics during middle school and later computer engieering at high school. For college I went to the University of North Carolina at Charlotte where I initially studied computer science but switched to biology my junior year. I would later find out that year that I had ADHD and that had been a major component to why I hadn't done well in computer science. With the help I recieved from my diagnosis I was able focus and improve my grades in my new major and thought I could just as easily enjoy a career in it.


After graduating I worked in a couple laboratories until I could get an environmentally related job. Once there I found that I couldn't see myself working in the field long-term. On the side I had continued to program as I still enjoyed it and eventually decided to change my career by enrolling a web development course at Tech Talent South (TTS). There I was able to build a solid foundation in web development as well as gain a sense of direction as to where I wanted to go with coding.


I'm very excited for what the future holds for me. Since graduating the course I've been learning every day to build web applications to expand my knowledge and gain the experiance needed to succeed in this field. This site is an example of that and a small one at that. I hope you'll look at my other projects that I've created as well!
